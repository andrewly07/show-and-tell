Andrew Ly's Show and Tell

I'm a collector.

This has always been a passion and hobby of mine for as long as I can remember.

I want anything that's exclusive or rare, from clothing, art, figures, NFTs and comics—you name it!

Specifically, the hunt for the next great find is what I love most about collecting. Whether I'm at a dusty old comic book store in the middle of nowhere, scrolling through an online auction, or sifting through bins at a collectibles show, there’s an undeniable thrill in the search.

But it's not just about what I'm looking for; it's about the entire experience. From the smell of old paper, the shared passion with other collectors, and the unique stories behind each piece. When I finally find that rare art piece or that classic comic book, it feels like a victory.

I suppose they’re a tangible connection to the things I love, and a way to hold on to a bit of nostalgia. For me, collecting is a hobby that’s as much about the past as it is about the present, and it brings me a lot of joy.

My most recent collecting obsession is grading & selling PSA cards

I've collected some Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, and One Piece trading cards. I'd highly recommend PSA or BGS as your third-party grading service as they are the most reputable.

Here are a few gems from my personal collection!

PSA 7 1st edition Slowking #14

This card is valued at around $130 - $150. Which isn't bad for a 1st edition copy of an older set, but the PSA 10 version was recently sold for $12,500!

PSA 10 Valkyrion the Magna Warrior (SDD-001)

This is valued roughly ~$240 as a PSA 10, but considering I bought it on eBay for under $20, it's a pretty fair trade in my opinion.

PSA 10 Dodgers x One Piece Night

This was from an exclusive event that I was able to pick up at the Dodgers stadium. It's the most valuable card currently in my collection currently valued around $1,600

As the card collecting craze continues to grow, I have no doubt that the price of my collection will as well (I don't encourage gambling, but if you're going to gamble anyway might as well do it on something you love).
